Common Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED

DO I NEED TO BOOK A TOUR? +

While we welcome walk-ins during staffed hours, we recommend booking a tour so a membership advisor can dedicate time to show you the facility and discuss your goals.

WHAT ARE YOUR STAFFED HOURS? +

Our facility is open 24/7 for members with keycard access. Staff are available from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily for assistance, shakes, and merchandise.

IS PERSONAL TRAINING INCLUDED? +

Personal training packages are sold separately or as part of our Platinum Membership. All new members receive one complimentary induction session.

CAN I FREEZE MY MEMBERSHIP? +

Yes, all memberships can be frozen for up to 30 days per year at no additional charge. Simply contact our front desk or use the Ascend app to manage your membership.
